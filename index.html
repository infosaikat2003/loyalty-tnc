Machli Wallah's Store - Loyalty Points Terms & Conditions

1. Earning Loyalty Points

  • Customers earn 1 loyalty point for every ₹99 spent on eligible transactions.
  • A minimum invoice value of ₹400 is required to earn points.
  • Points will be credited only after a successful payment.
  • Points are not earned on taxes, shipping charges, or already discounted purchases.
  • Loyalty points may not be awarded for bulk purchases with special discounts.

2. Redemption of Loyalty Points

  • 1 loyalty point = ₹0.10 discount.
  • Maximum redemption allowed per invoice: 10% of the invoice value.
  • Minimum invoice value to redeem points: ₹489.
  • Points cannot be combined with other promotional offers unless specified.

3. Expiry & Validity

  • Points expire 366 days after issuance.
  • Expired points cannot be reissued.

4. Refunds & Cancellations

  • If an order is canceled, the points earned will also be deducted.
  • If points were redeemed on a refunded order, the equivalent amount will be adjusted.

5. General Terms & Conditions

  • Loyalty points have no cash value and cannot be converted into real money.
  • Points are non-transferable between customer accounts.
  • Machli Wallah's Store reserves the right to modify or terminate the loyalty program at any time.
  • Disputes regarding points will be resolved at the sole discretion of Machli Wallah’s Store.
  • By participating in the loyalty program, customers agree to these terms and conditions.
